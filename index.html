What Is The Ozone Convention
A global platform for ozone therapy education

The Ozone Convention was created with a simple idea: make the world's most important ozone therapy knowledge accessible to anyone, anywhere, at a price that doesn't get in the way of learning.

Our online events bring together leading practitioners, researchers, and educators — covering ozone therapy and its most powerful supporting therapies. Two levels ensure that both beginners and experienced practitioners find exactly what they need.

Our Story
From idea to global community

The Ozone Convention was born from a simple frustration: the most important knowledge in ozone therapy was locked behind expensive conferences, scattered across countries, and inaccessible to most people who needed it.

Founded by Tito De Hoyos — a technologist and long-time member of the ozone therapy community — the convention set out to change that. Two successful online events in 2021 and 2022 brought together dozens of the world's most respected practitioners, reaching attendees from every continent.

In 2023, the convention had to pause due to serious personal circumstances — including family health crises that required Tito's full attention. But the mission never changed. We are working to bring it back — bigger, better, and more accessible than ever.
